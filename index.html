Our Story

Built by
Nigerians,
for Nigerians.

“We started VNS because we were tired of watching friends lose their vehicles with no way to find them. We knew technology could change that.”

Verified Network Solutions was founded in Lagos in 2022 with a single goal: give every Nigerian vehicle owner the same level of protection that multinational fleets enjoy. We started with two technicians, a van, and a belief that affordable, professional-grade tracking would transform how people protect what matters most.

Today we operate across 18 Nigerian cities, with a team of certified installation engineers and a customer success rate that speaks for itself. Every tracker we install. Every dash cam we fit. Every fleet we manage. All backed by the same promise: as sure as the sunrise.
